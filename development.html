Development in Cuttack

Cuttack is rapidly developing as a modern city while preserving its rich heritage. The city has seen significant progress in infrastructure, healthcare, education, and urban planning, making it a vibrant center for growth in Odisha.

Barabati Sports Complex

A major sports hub, hosting national and international cricket matches, and promoting sports development in the region.

SCB Medical Expansion

Major upgrades and expansion projects are transforming SCB Medical College into a world-class healthcare and research institution.

Omfed Factory

This ambitious project aims to modernize and expand the OMFED factory, enhancing production facilities and promoting dairy-based industries to boost employment and rural economy.

Smart City Initiatives

Cuttack is implementing smart city solutions, including better roads, digital services, waste management, and green spaces for a sustainable future.

These development projects are shaping Cuttack into a modern, inclusive, and sustainable city, while honoring its historical and cultural legacy.

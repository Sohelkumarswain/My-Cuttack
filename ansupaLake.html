Ansupa Lake

Ansupa Lake, located on the banks of the Mahanadi River, is the largest freshwater lake in Odisha. It is renowned for its picturesque landscape, rich biodiversity, and as a haven for migratory birds. Visitors can enjoy boating, bird watching, and relaxing amidst lush greenery. The lake is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, photographers, and those seeking tranquility away from the city bustle.

  • Location: Banki, Cuttack district, Odisha
  • Activities: Boating, Bird Watching, Nature Walks
  • Best Time to Visit: Winter (Nov-Feb) for migratory birds

Education in Cuttack

Cuttack is renowned as an educational hub of Odisha, home to some of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in the state. The city boasts a rich legacy in the field of education, with a vibrant academic environment that attracts students from across the region.

Ravenshaw University

Established in 1868, Ravenshaw is one of India's oldest and most respected universities, known for its academic excellence and historic campus.

SCB Medical College

A premier medical institution, SCB Medical College is a center of excellence for medical education and research in eastern India.

National Law University Odisha (NLUO)

A leading law university offering top-notch legal education and research opportunities.

sri sri university, Cuttack

One of the oldest schools in Odisha, providing quality education since 1882.

Cuttack is also home to several engineering, management, and professional colleges, making it a preferred destination for higher education. The city's educational institutions have produced many distinguished alumni who have excelled in various fields, contributing to the intellectual and cultural growth of Odisha and India.

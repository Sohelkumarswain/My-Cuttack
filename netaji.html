History of Netaji Birth Place

Early Life & Birthplace

Date and Place of Birth: Subhas Chandra Bose was born on 23 January 1897 in Cuttack, which at the time was part of the Bengal Presidency, British India. Family: His parents were Janakinath Bose (father) and Prabhavati Devi (mother). Janakinath was a lawyer and well-respected figure in Cuttack.

Childhood & Early Years

Netaji spent the early part of his life (about first 16 years) at his ancestral home in Cuttack, before the family shifted to Calcutta (now Kolkata). He attended school locally: first at a Protestant European School (Stewart School) for elementary education, then matriculated at Ravenshaw Collegiate School in Cuttack.

Conversion into a Museum

The home was officially converted into the Netaji Birth Place Museum (or Netaji Museum) around 2007.
